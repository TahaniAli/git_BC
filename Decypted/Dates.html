About The Use Case


Dates Supply Chain Management Based on IoT and Blockchain

Subtitle

The solution is to track the dates from the farm to the customer by using an RFID tag that is attached to the box containing the dates. The history that the product went through will be recorded as transaction on blockchain. To ensure the quality of the product in case of any damage. We can rely on the blockchain history to detect the exact stage that the damage occured.
